Abstract 摘要

When experiencing negative emotions, individuals often reach out for social support to help regulate their emotions. In times of an acute crisis, however, close friends might not be available, and physical closeness might be impossible. This functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) study investigated the effect of social proximity on the effectiveness of social support for regulating emotions and the underlying neural mechanisms. Participants regulated their emotions in response to negative images either alone (intrapersonal regulation), or with help of a picture and supporting sentence provided by the best friend, or by a stranger (interpersonal regulation). Regulation success was enhanced for the support of friends compared to regulating alone or with the support of strangers. This effect was accompanied by the interplay of large-scale brain networks involved in processing emotions, social cognition, and cognitive control. Interpersonal regulation appeared to be implemented by lateral prefrontal regions. The amygdala showed increased activation for strangers. The activation profile of the social cognition network suggests a role in supporting empathic and mentalizing processes. The results highlight the power of social connectedness for boosting emotion regulation ability and the different neural networks that contribute to this effect.
当遇到负面情绪时，人们通常会寻求社会支持来帮助调节情绪。然而，在发生严重危机时，亲近的朋友可能不在身边，而身体上的亲近也可能是不可能的。这项功能性磁共振成像（fMRI）研究调查了社会亲近度对社会支持调节情绪的有效性的影响及其潜在的神经机制。受试者在面对负面图像时，可以单独调节情绪（个人内部调节），也可以在好友或陌生人提供的图片和辅助句子的帮助下调节情绪（人际调节）。与单独调节或在陌生人的帮助下调节相比，在朋友的帮助下调节的成功率更高。这种效应伴随着涉及情绪处理、社会认知和认知控制的大规模大脑网络的相互作用。人际关系调节似乎是由外侧前额叶区域实施的。杏仁核对陌生人的激活增加。社会认知网络的激活特征表明，它在支持移情和心智化过程中发挥了作用。研究结果凸显了社会联系在提高情绪调节能力方面的力量，以及促成这种效应的不同神经网络。

1. Introduction 1. 导言

In emotionally challenging situations, such as being in lock-down during the COVID-19 pandemic, people often seek out support from family, friends, partners, or even strangers (e.g., via helplines) with the goal to alter one's affective state (Rime, 2009), or to dampen stress (Uchino and Garvey, 1997). For many people in lock-down, social support is limited to online contact thereby lacking physical closeness. However, even outside the context of the pandemic, relying on social support via video and chat functions has become increasingly common. Regulating one's own emotions through social interaction using empathic, supportive, and prosocial behaviors is referred to as interpersonal emotion regulation (Dixon-Gordon et al., 2015; Niven, 2017; Zaki and Williams, 2013). In contrast to intrapersonal emotion regulation (i.e. regulating emotions without others), little attention has been devoted to the efficacy and neural underpinnings of this process.
在具有情感挑战的情况下，例如在 COVID-19 大流行期间处于封锁状态，人们通常会向家人、朋友、伴侣甚至陌生人（例如通过求助热线）寻求支持，目的是改变自己的情感状态（Rime，2009 年）或减轻压力（Uchino 和 Garvey，1997 年）。对于许多被禁闭的人来说，社会支持仅限于在线联系，因此缺乏身体上的亲近感。然而，即使在大流行病之外，通过视频和聊天功能获得社会支持也变得越来越普遍。通过社会交往使用移情、支持和亲社会行为来调节自己的情绪被称为人际情绪调节（Dixon-Gordon 等人，2015；Niven，2017；Zaki 和 Williams，2013）。与人际情绪调节（即在没有他人在场的情况下调节情绪）相比，人们很少关注这一过程的功效和神经基础。

Neurally, Reek et al. (2016) proposed a framework for the implementation of interpersonal emotion regulation in the brain, which is based on three neural systems: (1) a cognitive control system, (2) social cognition and empathy system, and (3) an emotion generation system. These systems reflect the interactive nature of interpersonal emotion regulation. According to Zaki and Williams (2013), interpersonal emotion regulation can be characterized by an “observer” supporting a “target” in their attempt to change their emotional experience. The observer responds to the emotional response of the target and attempts to regulate the target's emotions via strategies to enhance cognitive control (Reek et al., 2016). In the target, this should involve the neural cognitive control system (system I), including the dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (dlPFC), ventrolateral prefrontal cortex (vlPFC), and anterior cingulate cortex (ACC) as well as the orbitofrontal cortex (OFC) and parietal cortex (e.g., Morawetz et al., 2017b, 2020); in addition, the involvement of the social cognition system (system II) related to empathy and mentalizing would occur, including dorsal premotor regions, temporo-parietal junction (TPJ), dorsal medial prefrontal cortex (dmPFC) and precuneus (Bernhardt and Singer, 2012; Decety and Jackson, 2004; Jauniaux et al., 2019). The target's emotional response is based on the emotion-generation system (system III), which involves the amygdala and ventral striatum (Ochsner et al., 2012). The amygdala has also been suggested to be involved in social perception (detecting, decoding, and interpreting of social signals), social affiliation (motivating prosocial or affiliative behaviors), and regulating interpersonal distance (Bickart et al., 2014; Kennedy et al., 2009; Laird et al., 2015).
在神经系统方面，Reek 等人（2016）提出了人际情绪调节在大脑中的实现框架，该框架基于三个神经系统：（1）认知控制系统；（2）社会认知和移情系统；（3）情绪生成系统。这些系统反映了人际情绪调节的互动性。根据 Zaki 和 Williams（2013 年）的研究，人际情绪调节的特点是 "观察者" 支持 "目标" 试图改变他们的情绪体验。观察者对目标的情绪反应做出反应，并试图通过加强认知控制的策略来调节目标的情绪（Reek 等人，2016 年）。在目标身上，这应该涉及神经认知控制系统（系统 I），包括背外侧前额叶皮层（dlPFC）、腹外侧前额叶皮层（vlPFC）、前扣带回皮层（ACC）以及眶额皮层（OFC）和顶叶皮层（例如，Morawetz et al、2017b，2020）；此外，与移情和心智化有关的社会认知系统（系统 II）也会参与其中，包括背侧前运动区、颞顶交界处（TPJ）、背侧内侧前额叶皮层（dmPFC）和楔前区（Bernhardt 和 Singer，2012；Decety 和 Jackson，2004；Jauniaux 等人，2019）。目标的情绪反应基于情绪生成系统（系统 III），该系统涉及杏仁核和腹侧纹状体（Ochsner 等人，2012 年）。杏仁核还被认为参与了社会感知（检测、解码和解释社会信号）、社会归属（激励亲社会或归属行为）以及调节人际距离（Bickart 等人，2014 年；Kennedy 等人，2009 年；Laird 等人，2015 年）。

In this study, we focused on the effect of social proximity on interpersonal emotion regulation. Previous research demonstrated that the presence of a close other diminished negative affect and attenuated activity in a network associated with salience, vigilance, and regulatory self-control (i.e., dlPFC, vlPFC, posterior parietal cortex, and dorsal ACC), whereas threat-related activity was increased when a person was accompanied by a stranger or when alone (Coan et al., 2006, 2013, 2017; Kawamichi et al., 2015). These findings have been discussed in light of social baseline theory, which proposes that the presence of other people helps individuals to conserve important and often metabolically costly somatic and neural resources through the social regulation of emotion (Beckes and Coan, 2011). These results suggest that neural activation in lateral PFC regions, parietal, and cingulate cortex during interpersonal emotion regulation might depend on the proximity to the observer, i.e. the person providing emotional support.
在本研究中，我们重点研究了社会亲近感对人际情绪调节的影响。以往的研究表明，当有亲密的人在场时，消极情绪会减弱，与突出性、警觉性和调节性自我控制相关的网络（即顶叶皮层、后顶叶皮层和背侧 ACC）的活动也会减弱，而当一个人有陌生人陪伴或独自一人时，与威胁相关的活动会增加（Coan 等人，2006 年、2013 年、2017 年；Kawamichi 等人，2015 年）。社会基线理论认为，他人的存在有助于个体通过情绪的社会调控来保存重要的、通常需要付出高昂代谢代价的躯体和神经资源（Beckes 和 Coan，2011 年）。这些结果表明，在人际情绪调节过程中，外侧前额叶皮层区域、顶叶和扣带回皮层的神经激活可能取决于与观察者（即提供情绪支持的人）的距离。

The current study examined the neural mechanisms underpinning interpersonal emotion regulation initiated remotely by observers of different social closeness using functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) and univariate as well as multivariate analyses approaches. The targets used reappraisal to down-regulate their emotions in response to negative pictures. In three regulation conditions, support (i.e. interpersonal emotion regulation) was provided by an observer, who was either the best friend, or a stranger, or no support was provided (intrapersonal emotion regulation). We predicted that social proximity (in the absence of physical closeness) would make a significant contribution to reducing negative affect and that this would be accompanied by an increase in activity in neural circuits associated with emotion regulation and social cognition (system I and system II), especially in the lateral prefrontal cortex (dlPFC and vlPFC) and parietal cortex (TPJ). For strangers, the same (potentially weaker) advantage in emotion regulation was predicted as for close friends (Coan et al., 2006). Alternatively, however, strangers might trigger a stress response (Coan et al., 2017), which could be counterproductive and might lead to increased activation in the amygdala (system III).
本研究采用功能磁共振成像（fMRI）和单变量及多变量分析方法，研究了不同社会亲密度的观察者远程启动人际情绪调节的神经机制。观察对象在看到负面图片时，会通过重新评价来降低自己的情绪。在三种调节条件下，观察者（最好的朋友或陌生人）提供支持（即人际情绪调节）或不提供支持（人内情绪调节）。我们预测，社交亲近（在没有身体亲近的情况下）将对减少负面情绪做出重大贡献，与此同时，与情绪调节和社会认知相关的神经回路（系统 I 和系统 II）的活动也会增加，尤其是在外侧前额叶皮层（dlPFC 和 vlPFC）和顶叶皮层（TPJ）。对于陌生人，情绪调节方面的优势预计与亲密朋友相同（可能较弱）（Coan 等人，2006 年）。不过，陌生人也可能引发应激反应（Coan 等人，2017 年），这可能会适得其反，并可能导致杏仁核（系统 III）的激活增加。

2. Methods 2. 方法

Here we report two studies: a behavioral pilot experiment (that was conducted few months before the fMRI experiment) and an fMRI experiment to investigate the effect of social proximity on emotion regulation, which served to replicate the effects of the behavioral pilot experiment and investigate the neural correlates of this process. The two experiments used identical experimental paradigms with two independent samples and are therefore described together in this section. Session 1 was identical for both experiments, while session 2 took either place in a behavioral testing room in front of a computer (behavioral pilot experiment) or inside the MRI scanner (fMRI experiment).
在此，我们报告了两项研究：一项行为先导实验（在 fMRI 实验前几个月进行）和一项 fMRI 实验，旨在研究社会亲近感对情绪调节的影响，该实验复制了行为先导实验的效果，并研究了这一过程的神经相关性。这两项实验使用了相同的实验范式和两个独立的样本，因此在本节中一并介绍。两个实验的第 1 个环节完全相同，而第 2 个环节要么在行为测试室的电脑前进行（行为先导实验），要么在核磁共振成像扫描仪内进行（fMRI 实验）。

2.1. Participants 2.1. 与会者

Participants in both experiments gave written, informed consent to participate. The studies were approved by the local ethics committee of Freie Universität Berlin.
两项实验的参与者均在知情的情况下书面同意参与实验。研究获得了柏林自由大学当地伦理委员会的批准。

Behavioral pilot experiment: We tested 32 right-handed, healthy participants with normal or corrected to normal vision (28 female; mean age = 23.69 years, SD = 3.77). 22 participants had a best friend of the same sex and 10 participants of the opposite sex. (Note that we did not conduct a formal power analysis prior to the behavioural pilot study, but we aimed for ~30 pilot participants to establish whether an effect was likely to exist, after which we progressed with the fMRI study.)
行为试点实验：我们测试了 32 名视力正常或矫正为正常的右利手健康参与者（28 名女性；平均年龄 = 23.69 岁，SD = 3.77）。22 名参与者有一位同性好友，10 名参与者有一位异性好友（请注意，我们在行为试验研究之前没有进行正式的功率分析，但我们的目标是约 30 名试验参与者，以确定是否可能存在效应，之后我们将继续进行 fMRI 研究）。

fMRI experiment: We tested 38 right-handed, healthy participants with normal or corrected to normal vision. One participant was excluded due to technical problems with data acquisition. The final sample consisted of 37 participants (31 female; mean age = 22 years, SD = 2.58). The best friend was in 29 cases of the same sex and 8 cases of the opposite sex.
fMRI 实验：我们对 38 名视力正常或矫正为正常的右利手健康参与者进行了测试。由于数据采集的技术问题，一名参与者被排除在外。最终样本包括 37 名参与者（31 名女性；平均年龄 = 22 岁，SD = 2.58）。最好的朋友中有 29 人是同性，8 人是异性。

2.2.1. Instruction statements for interpersonal conditions
2.2.1. 人际关系条件的指令说明

Seventy-two different German statements (adapted from Xie et al., 2016) were used to instruct reappraisal for the interpersonal conditions (Friend & Stranger). The statements included 6 different tactics (McRae et al., 2012):
72 个不同的德国语句（改编自 Xie 等人，2016 年）被用于指导人际条件（朋友和陌生人）下的重新评估。这些语句包括 6 种不同的策略（麦克雷等人，2012 年）：

  • (1)

    “situation-based: reality challenge” (e.g., “This is not real.”),
    "基于情境：现实挑战"（如 "这不是真的"）、

  • (2)

    “distancing” (e.g., “This doesn't affect you.”),
    "疏远"（例如，"这对你没有影响"）、

  • (3)

    “acceptance” (e.g., “Life goes on.”),
    "接受"（如 "生活还要继续"）、

  • (4)

    “situation-based: change future consequences” (e.g., “The situation will improve with time.”),
    "基于情况：改变未来后果"（如 "随着时间的推移，情况会有所改善"）、

  • (5)

    “explicitly positive” (e.g., “Everything will be fine.”), and
    "明确肯定"（如 "一切都会好起来的"），以及

  • (6)

    “problem-solving” (e.g., “Calm down.”).
    "解决问题"（如 "冷静下来"）。

These reappraisal statements were rated online by an independent sample (n = 35; 30 female; mean age = 28.88 years, SD = 9.04) on valence (“How do you feel about this statement?”), arousal (“How arousing is this statement”) and social proximity (“How close would you feel to someone who would say this to you?”) on a Likert scale from 1 to 100 (very negative/very calm/very distant to very positive/very exciting/very close). Half of the statements used a formal way of addressing participants (i.e. the German “Sie”, used to address unfamiliar people) during the Stranger condition and half an informal way (i.e. the German “Du”, used to address friends and close acquaintances) during the Friend condition, while the content and wording of the statements were kept identical. This manipulation was used to increase the ecological validity of the experiment. Overall, statements using a formal way of address were rated as more distant compared to statements using an informal way of instruction (t(34) = −6.36, p < 0.001, Cohen's d = 1.49). However, the different ways of being addressed did not affect the perceived valence (formal vs. informal: t(34) = −1.68, p = 0.102, Cohen's d = 0.2) or arousal (formal vs. informal: t(34) = 0.63, p = 0.53, Cohen's d = −0.07) of the statements, which were rated as relatively neutral on average on both dimensions (mean valence = 48.85, SD = 8.49; mean arousal = 47.98, SD = 7.99). Hence, the two ways of addressing people only affected social proximity, and they were used to create the interpersonal emotion regulation conditions – Stranger and Friend.
独立样本（n = 35；30 位女性；平均年龄 = 28.88 岁，SD = 9.04）在网上对这些重新评价语句的情绪（"您对这句话有什么感觉？"）、唤醒（"这句话有多唤醒您"）和社会亲近感（"如果有人对您说这句话，您会觉得他与您有多亲近？"）进行了评价，评价采用李克特量表，从 1 到 100（从非常消极 / 非常平静 / 非常疏远到非常积极 / 非常兴奋 / 非常亲近）。在 "陌生人" 条件下，一半的语句使用正式称呼参与者的方式（即德语 "Sie"，用于称呼不熟悉的人）；在 "朋友" 条件下，一半的语句使用非正式称呼参与者的方式（即德语 "Du"，用于称呼朋友和熟人），而语句的内容和措辞则保持一致。这一操作是为了提高实验的生态效度。总体而言，与使用非正式称呼方式的陈述相比，使用正式称呼方式的陈述被评为更疏远（t(34) = -6.36，p < 0.001，Cohen's d = 1.49）。然而，不同的称呼方式并不影响人们对陈述的感知价值（正式与非正式：t(34) = -1.68, p = 0.102, Cohen's d = 0.2）或唤醒程度（正式与非正式：t(34) = 0.63, p = 0.53, Cohen's d = -0.07），这两个维度的平均评价都相对中性（平均价值 = 48.85, SD = 8.49；平均唤醒程度 = 47.98, SD = 7.99）。因此，这两种称呼方式只影响社会亲近度，它们被用来创建人际情绪调节条件 -- 陌生人和朋友。

2.2.2. Emotional stimuli & pictures for regulation conditions
2.2.2. 调节条件下的情绪刺激和图片

Stimuli consisted of 144 aversive pictures from the International Affective Picture System (IAPS) (Bradley and Lang, 2007) according to the normative ratings, which are available on a Likert scale from 1 (very negative/very calm) to 9 (very positive/very arousing): mean valence = 2.85, SD = 0.56, mean arousal = 5.65, SD = 0.78. The stimulus set was divided into four sets of 36 images that were matched in content, valence, and arousal across the four experimental conditions to ensure that emotion induction was comparable.
刺激物由 144 幅国际情感图片系统（IAPS）（Bradley 和 Lang，2007 年）中的厌恶图片组成，根据标准评分标准，这些图片可使用李克特量表从 1（非常消极 / 非常平静）到 9（非常积极 / 非常亢奋）进行评分：平均情绪 = 2.85，标准差 = 0.56，平均亢奋 = 5.65，标准差 = 0.78。为了确保情绪诱导的可比性，刺激集被分为四组，每组 36 张图片，四种实验条件下的图片在内容、情绪和唤醒程度上都是匹配的。

Digital photos of the best friends were taken (in a black t-shirt against a white wall, covering the face to mid-chest, without jewelry), converted into black and white images, and used in the Friend condition. In addition, a picture of a female or male person (unknown to the participant) was used in the Stranger condition. The sex of the stranger was matched to the best friend to make the interpersonal emotion regulation conditions comparable and to reduce possible gender effects. Scrambled versions of these pictures were created and presented during the non-social conditions (Look, which was the control condition, and the intrapersonal condition, here simply referred to as Decrease) to match the trial structure of the interpersonal conditions.
拍摄好朋友的数码照片（穿着黑色 T 恤，靠着白墙，遮住脸部至胸部中间，不戴首饰），转换成黑白图片，用于 "朋友" 条件。此外，在 "陌生人" 条件中还使用了一张女性或男性（参与者不认识）的照片。陌生人的性别与最好朋友的性别相匹配，以使人际情绪调节条件具有可比性，并减少可能的性别效应。为了与人际情绪调节条件的试验结构相匹配，我们制作了这些图片的乱码版本，并在非社交条件（"看"，即对照条件；人际情绪调节条件，在此简称为 "减少"）中呈现。

2.2.3. 刺激演示

The stimulus presentation and response recording were controlled by Presentation (Version 14.1, Neurobehavioral Systems, USA). Inside the MRI scanner, visual stimuli were presented in the center of the screen using dual-display goggles (VisuaStim, MR Research, USA).
刺激呈现和反应记录由 Presentation（14.1 版，美国神经行为系统公司）控制。在核磁共振扫描仪内，使用双显示护目镜（VisuaStim，MR Research，美国）在屏幕中央显示视觉刺激。

2.3. Experimental cover story: session 1
2.3. 实验封面故事：第 1 节

During recruitment, participants were informed that the experiment would take place over two different days, and they were asked to bring their best friend to the lab for the first session. Best friends were explicitly defined as not having a sexual relationship with each other, i.e. no romantic partners. Upon arriving for session 1, participants and best friends were told to fill out a set of questionnaires on a computer in separate rooms to find out more about their friendship and how they support each other in stressful situations. We told both of them that this information would be used to modify the experiment for session 2. Also, the best friend was instructed to provide five supporting statements for the participant that could be used in emotionally difficult situations and was told that these statements would be used in the fMRI experiment (session 2). Participants were told that they would perform an emotion regulation task with the help of their best friend or a stranger during session 2. Of note, participants were told that a stranger also provided similar statements, which would be used in the experiment. This procedure was identical for the behavioral pilot experiment and the fMRI experiment.
在招募过程中，参与者被告知实验将在两个不同的日子里进行，并要求他们带自己最好的朋友来实验室参加第一次实验。最好的朋友被明确定义为彼此没有性关系，即没有恋爱伴侣。到达第一阶段后，参与者和最好的朋友被告知要在不同房间的电脑上填写一组问卷，以了解他们的友谊以及他们在压力情况下如何相互支持。我们告诉他们，这些信息将用于修改第 2 个环节的实验。此外，我们还指示最好的朋友为受试者提供五句在情绪困难情况下可以使用的支持性语句，并告诉受试者这些语句将用于 fMRI 实验（环节 2）。参与者被告知，在第二环节中，他们将在好友或陌生人的帮助下完成一项情绪调节任务。值得注意的是，参与者被告知陌生人也提供了类似的陈述，这些陈述将在实验中使用。行为先导实验和 fMRI 实验的程序完全相同。

2.4. Procedure: session 2
2.4. 程序：环节 2

During session 2, participants were given instructions for and training on the experimental task they would perform. The training consisted of 8 trials (2 trials per condition) to familiarise the participants with the trial design and the emotional state rating scale.
在第 2 个环节中，参与者将接受实验任务的指导和培训。培训包括 8 次试验（每次 2 个条件），目的是让受试者熟悉试验设计和情绪状态评分量表。

In the experiment, a standard emotion regulation task was used, which was adapted from previous studies (Morawetz et al., 2017a, 2016a, 2016b). In each trial, participants were asked to either regulate their emotions in response to viewing an aversive picture or to attentively view the picture in the control condition. Four task conditions were implemented (Fig. 1A): (1) In the Look condition, participants were first presented with a scrambled image and underneath the instruction to view the following aversive image attentively and allow themselves to experience/feel any emotional responses, which it might elicit without manipulating their emotions. This constituted the control condition. (2) In the Decrease condition, participants viewed a scrambled image along with the instruction to actively reduce the intensity of negative emotions. Importantly, no specific tactic was instructed, just a general statement to down-regulate their emotions was presented. During the training, participants were told that they could use any strategy that would help them to down-regulate their emotions e.g., by distancing themselves, reducing the personal relevance, etc. (Eippert et al., 2007; Ochsner et al., 2004; Urry et al., 2009). Importantly, participants were told not to substitute negative emotions with positive emotions as this would result in distraction from negative emotions rather than a reappraisal of the depicted situation (Webb et al., 2012). This condition was non-social and required intrapersonal emotion regulation. (3) In the Friend condition, participants viewed a photo of their best friend along with a regulation statement (using the informal form of addressing the participant) and were asked to use this sentence to decrease their emotions in response to the subsequently presented aversive image. (4) In the Stranger condition, participants viewed a photo of a stranger along with a matched statement (using the formal form of addressing the participant) and were asked to use this sentence to decrease their emotions in response to the subsequently displayed negative image. The Friend and Stranger conditions represented the social, interpersonal emotion regulation conditions.
实验中使用了一项标准的情绪调节任务，该任务改编自之前的研究（莫拉维兹等人，2017a, 2016a, 2016b）。在每次试验中，参与者被要求在观看厌恶图片时调节情绪，或者在控制条件下专注地观看图片。共实施了四种任务条件（图 1A）：(1) 在观察条件下，参与者首先会看到一张乱码图片，然后在指示下专注地观看下面的厌恶图片，并允许自己体验 / 感受图片可能引起的任何情绪反应，而不操纵自己的情绪。这就是控制条件。(2）在 "减少" 条件下，受试者在观看乱码图像的同时，还要接受积极减少负面情绪强度的指令。重要的是，并没有指示具体的策略，只是呈现了一个降低调节情绪的一般性声明。在训练过程中，参与者被告知他们可以使用任何能帮助他们降低情绪的策略，例如，通过拉开距离、降低个人相关性等（Eippert 等人，2007 年；Ochsner 等人，2004 年；Urry 等人，2009 年）。重要的是，参与者被告知不要用积极情绪代替消极情绪，因为这样做会分散消极情绪的注意力，而不是重新评估所描述的情况（Webb 等人，2012 年）。这一条件是非社会性的，需要个人内部的情绪调节。(3）在 "朋友" 条件下，被试在观看自己最好朋友的照片时，会同时看到一句调节语句（使用非正式的称呼形式），被试被要求用这句话来降低自己对随后出现的厌恶图像的情绪反应。(4）在 "陌生人" 条件下，受试者观看一张陌生人的照片，并同时观看一份与之相匹配的陈述（使用正式的称呼形式），受试者被要求使用这句话来减少他们对随后出现的负面图像的情绪反应。朋友和陌生人条件代表了社会、人际情绪调节条件。

Each trial started with a regulation statement underneath the presentation of the respective picture (5 s) indicating the experimental condition. To further increase or decrease social proximity, the name of the friend or the stranger was presented along with the photo (for example: Laura says: “Keep calm.”). This was followed by a fixation cross for a jittered duration of 2–6 s. Subsequently, an aversive image was presented for 8 s during which the instructed strategy had to be applied. Again, this was followed by a fixation cross for a jittered duration of 2–6 s. After this, participants were asked to rate their current emotional state (from “very negative” to “very positive”; arbitrarily scaled off-line from −200 to +200 for the analyses) using a two-button fiber optic response pad (fORP, Cambridge Research Systems Ltd.). The response window was 4 s. Finally, a central fixation cross was presented for a jittered duration of 2–6 s, concluding the trial.
每次试验开始时，在展示相应照片的下方（5 秒钟）都会有一个说明实验条件的规定语句。为了进一步增加或减少社交亲近感，朋友或陌生人的名字会与照片一起出现（例如：劳拉说："保持冷静"）。随后，一张厌恶的图片会出现 8 秒钟，在此期间必须使用指示策略。之后，参与者被要求使用双键光纤反应板（fORP，剑桥研究系统有限公司）对其当前的情绪状态进行评分（从 "非常消极" 到 "非常积极"；为便于分析，离线比例为 - 200 到 +200）。反应窗口为 4 秒钟。最后，中央固定十字架会闪烁 2-6 秒钟，从而结束试验。

Participants performed six runs. Each run consisted of 24 trials, containing images that were balanced concerning content, valence, and arousal. The order of aversive images and conditions was pseudo-randomized within runs. One trial lasted 29 s on average, one run lasted about 12 min. The experiment consisted of 144 trials and was either conducted in front of a computer (behavioral pilot experiment) or inside the MRI scanner (fMRI experiment). The behavioral pilot experiment lasted 1 h and the fMRI experiment resulted in ~1 h 12 min of scanning.
受试者共进行了六次测试。每次运行由 24 个试验组成，其中包含的图像在内容、价值和唤醒程度方面都是均衡的。在运行过程中，厌恶图像和条件的顺序是伪随机的。一次试验平均持续 29 秒，一次运行持续约 12 分钟。实验由 144 次试验组成，在计算机前（行为先导实验）或核磁共振成像扫描仪内（fMRI 实验）进行。行为先导实验持续了 1 小时，fMRI 实验的扫描时间约为 1 小时 12 分钟。

2.5. 心理测量

在第一环节中，参与者完成了几份关于人内和人际情绪调节、友谊关系质量和人格特质的问卷（有关测量的详细说明，请参阅补充材料）。

2.6. fMRI data acquisition
2.6. fMRI 数据采集

A high-resolution 3D T1-weighted dataset was acquired for each subject (176 sagittal sections, 1 × 1 × 1 mm³; 256 × 256 data acquisition matrix). Functional images were acquired using a T2*-weighted, gradient-echo echo-planar imaging (EPI) pulse sequence recording 37 sections oriented parallel to the anterior and posterior commissure at an in-plane resolution of 3 × 3 × 3 mm³ (interslice gap = 0; TE = 30 ms; TR = 2 s; FA = 90°; FoV = 192 × 192 mm²; 64 × 64 data acquisition matrix). 2.7.1. 情绪状态评级

我们使用重复测量方差分析，然后进行 t 检验（使用 SPSS 25 版），以检验情绪调节的效果和情绪调节条件之间的差异。

2.7.2. fMRI data analysis
2.7.2. fMRI 数据分析

Preprocessing: Functional imaging data analysis was performed using SPM12 (Wellcome Centre for Human Neuroimaging, London, UK). As interleaved slice acquisition was used, slice time correction was included during the preprocessing of the fMRI data. In addition, standard preprocessing involved realignment to the mean image of the first run, spatial normalization to the standard EPI template (MNI template), and spatial smoothing with an 8 mm full-width at half-maximum (FWHM) isotopic Gaussian kernel.
预处理：功能成像数据分析使用 SPM12（英国伦敦威康人类神经成像中心）进行。由于采用了交错切片采集，因此在对 fMRI 数据进行预处理时包括了切片时间校正。此外，标准预处理还包括与第一次运行的平均图像重新对齐，与标准 EPI 模板（MNI 模板）进行空间归一化，以及使用 8 毫米半最大值全宽（FWHM）同位素高斯核进行空间平滑。

Univariate regions of interest (ROI) analyses: To test the modulating effect of social conditions on emotion regulation, ROI analyses were performed on regions that previously have been implicated in the cognitive control of emotions (Morawetz et al., 2017b). In accord with Reeck et al. (2016), we defined ROIs within three neural systems that have been suggested to support social regulation: (1) system I that supports regulation and cognitive control, (2) system II involved in empathy, social cognition and/or inferring mental states, and (3) system III that targets emotion generation. We selected our ROIs based on our meta-analysis (Morawetz et al., 2017b) using the contrast Reappraisal>Control condition (the control condition in the meta-analysis was defined as the condition in which no emotion regulation was applied) to generate ROIs implicated in system I (5 ROIs: bilateral dlPFC, bilateral vlPFC, SMA) and in system II (4 ROIs: bilateral SMG, left MTG, left MFG/SFG). Using the reverse contrast Control condition>Reappraisal resulted in the definition of ROIs part of system III (2 ROIs: bilateral amygdala) (for details see Table 1). Marsbar (Version 0.44) toolbox for SPM12 (Brett et al., 2002) was used to create all ROIs. For each ROI, we applied the contrasts as described above.
单变量感兴趣区（ROI）分析：为了检验社会条件对情绪调节的调节作用，我们对之前被认为与情绪的认知控制有关的区域进行了 ROI 分析（Morawetz 等人，2017b）。根据 Reeck 等人（2016）的研究，我们在三个被认为支持社会调节的神经系统中定义了 ROI：（1）支持调节和认知控制的系统 I，（2）参与移情、社会认知和 / 或推断心理状态的系统 II，以及（3）以情绪生成为目标的系统 III。我们根据荟萃分析（Morawetz et al.，2017b）选择了 ROIs，使用对比 Reappraisal>Control 条件（荟萃分析中的控制条件被定义为未应用情绪调节的条件），以生成与系统 I（5 个 ROIs：双侧 dlPFC、双侧 vlPFC、SMA）和系统 II（4 个 ROIs：双侧 SMG、左侧 MTG、左侧 MFG/SFG）相关的 ROIs。使用反向对比 "控制条件">"重评"，则定义了系统 III 的部分 ROI（2 个 ROI：双侧杏仁核）（详见表 1）。我们使用 SPM12 的 Marsbar（0.44 版）工具箱（Brett 等人，2002 年）创建了所有 ROI。对于每个 ROI，我们都应用了上述的对比度。

A general linear model approach was then used for all ROIs. The first-level fixed-effects model was estimated for each participant to identify neural networks supporting emotion regulation and included the following regressors: instruction cue (duration 5 s), emotion regulation phase split by emotion regulation condition (Look, Decrease, Friend, and Stranger) (duration 8 s), and emotional state rating phase (duration 4 s). This model included motion parameters as nuisance covariates. The regressors were convolved with a canonical form of the hemodynamic response function.
然后对所有 ROI 采用一般线性模型方法。对每个参与者都估算了一级固定效应模型，以确定支持情绪调节的神经网络，该模型包括以下回归因子：指令提示（持续时间 5 秒）、按情绪调节条件（看、减少、朋友和陌生人）划分的情绪调节阶段（持续时间 8 秒）和情绪状态评级阶段（持续时间 4 秒）。该模型包括作为干扰协变量的运动参数。回归因子与血液动力学响应函数的典型形式相卷积。

In a second-level random-effects group analysis the emotion regulation conditions were compared. Importantly, we used the regressors for the emotion regulation phase, not the cueing phase, meaning that any visual differences between instruction screens could not bias the activation observed. We computed contrast images of brain activations associated with (1) emotion regulation in general [Friend+Stranger+Decrease>Look], (2) interpersonal versus intrapersonal regulation [Friend+Stranger>Decrease], (3) interpersonal emotion regulation [Friend>Look; Stranger>Look; Friend>Decrease; Stranger>Decrease], (4) intrapersonal emotion regulation [Decrease>Look; Decrease>Friend; Decrease>Stranger], and (5) emotion generation [Look>Decrease; Look>Friend; Look>Stranger]. T-statistics for each voxel were thresholded at cluster-defining threshold p <0.001, corrected for multiple comparisons across the whole brain with family-wise error rate (FWE) at pFWE < 0.05. For completeness, we additionally conducted whole-brain analyses for all contrasts that can be found in the Supplementary Material.
在二级随机效应组分析中，我们对情绪调节条件进行了比较。重要的是，我们使用了情绪调节阶段的回归因子，而不是提示阶段的回归因子，这意味着指令屏幕之间的任何视觉差异都不会影响所观察到的激活。我们计算了与以下情况相关的大脑激活对比图像：(1) 一般情绪调节 [朋友 + 陌生人 + 减少> 看]；(2) 人际调节与人内调节 [朋友 + 陌生人> 减少]；(3) 人际情绪调节 [朋友> 看；陌生人>看；朋友>减少；陌生人>减少]，(4) 人内情绪调节 [减少> 看；减少>朋友；减少>陌生人]，(5) 情绪产生 [看> 减少；看>朋友；看>陌生人]。每个体素的 T 统计量均以聚类定义阈值 p < 0.001 为阈值，并对整个大脑的多重比较进行校正，族内误差率（FWE）为 p FWE < 0.05。为了完整起见，我们还对所有对比进行了全脑分析，这些分析可在补充材料中找到。

Multivariate region of interest (ROI) analyses: Given the increased sensitivity of MVPA compared to traditional mass-univariate approaches (Kriegeskorte and Bandettini, 2007; Woolgar et al., 2014), we applied MVPA to the predefined ROIs (Kriegeskorte et al., 2006) to find regions that differed in their fine-grained activation patterns with respect to social proximity (Stranger vs. Friend). In Decoding analysis I, we investigated regions that are generally involved in emotion regulation and asked whether these explicitly encoded social information about the source of emotional support. For this, we used the a priori defined ROIs from the meta-analysis on emotion regulation (Table 1; 11 ROIs). In Decoding analysis II, we additionally analyzed those regions from the present study that were found to be activated for interpersonal emotion regulation in the whole-brain univariate analyses, but showed no activation differences with respect to social proximity (i.e., interpersonal vs. intrapersonal regulation [Friend+Stranger > Decrease]; Supplementary Material, Figure S1B and Table S2; resulting in 3 ROIs: Precuenus, left SFG, and left MTG). We reasoned that despite similar overall activation strength, fine-grained activation patterns within these regions might nevertheless represent social proximity. For both sets of MVPA analyses, all ROIs were first transferred back into individual subject space and then used as masks for the respective images to extract the relevant voxels.
多变量感兴趣区（ROI）分析：与传统的大规模单变量方法相比，MVPA 的灵敏度更高（Kriegeskorte 和 Bandettini，2007 年；Woolgar 等人，2014 年），因此我们将 MVPA 应用于预定义的 ROI（Kriegeskorte 等人，2006 年），以寻找与社会亲近程度（陌生人与朋友）相关的细粒度激活模式不同的区域。在解码分析 I 中，我们研究了通常参与情绪调节的区域，并询问这些区域是否明确编码了关于情绪支持来源的社会信息。为此，我们使用了情绪调节荟萃分析中先验定义的 ROI（表 1；11 个 ROI）。在解码分析 II 中，我们还分析了本研究中那些在全脑单变量分析中被发现激活了人际情绪调节，但在社会接近性方面没有显示出激活差异的区域（即人际调节与人内调节 [朋友 + 陌生人> 减少]；补充材料，图 S1B 和表 S2；结果是 3 个 ROI：Precuenus、左侧 SFG 和左侧 MTG）。我们的理由是，尽管总体激活强度相似，但这些区域内细粒度的激活模式可能代表了社会接近性。对于两组 MVPA 分析，首先将所有 ROI 转回单个受试者空间，然后将其用作相应图像的掩码，以提取相关体素。

We analyzed both the cuing phase (when the photos were presented) as well as the implementation phase (when emotion regulation was performed). All MVPA analyses were conducted using the standard approach as implemented in The Decoding Toolbox (Hebart et al., 2015) by applying support vector machine classifiers interfacing LIBSVM (Chang & Lin, 2011). For this, non-smoothed and non-normalized data from each participant were used as input for an identical GLM as described above for the univariate analyses. All trials were included independent of regulation success. The resulting beta-images for each condition, representing the model fit for the respective task phase of interest at each voxel, in turn, served as input for the classification, and the ROI masks were applied to these images.
我们分析了提示阶段（展示照片时）和实施阶段（进行情绪调节时）。所有 MVPA 分析均采用解码工具箱（Hebart 等人，2015 年）中实施的标准方法，应用支持向量机分类器与 LIBSVM（Chang 和 Lin，2011 年）接口。为此，每个参与者的非平滑和非标准化数据都被用作与上述单变量分析相同的 GLM 的输入。所有试验都包括在内，与调节成功与否无关。每个条件下产生的β图像代表了每个体素上各自任务阶段的模型拟合，反过来作为分类的输入，ROI 掩码应用于这些图像。

Separate analyses were conducted for each ROI. First, for each participant, the parameter estimates from all voxels within the ROI for each run were transformed into pattern vectors, representing the spatial activation patterns associated with each regulation condition in the respective region. These pattern vectors were used as input for pattern classification analyses, conducted for each participant. Separate pattern classification analyses were conducted for each task phase (cueing and implementation), but since the general structure of the analysis was the same, it is described only once in the next section.
对每个 ROI 分别进行分析。首先，对每个参与者而言，每次运行时 ROI 内所有体素的参数估计值都会被转换成模式向量，代表各自区域内与每种调节条件相关的空间激活模式。这些模式向量被用作对每位参与者进行模式分类分析的输入。每个任务阶段（提示和执行）都进行了单独的模式分类分析，但由于分析的总体结构相同，因此在下一节中只介绍一次。

After extracting the pattern vectors, a linear support vector machine (SVM) was trained on the vectors from both conditions (Friend vs. Stranger) from all runs but one to estimate a decision boundary in N-dimensional space that distinguishes between spatial activation patterns associated with the two interpersonal regulation conditions. This is referred to as the “training data set”. The trained classifier was then tested on data from the independent left-out run, i.e. the “test data set”. A six-fold cross-validation was performed by repeating the classification process independently with the pattern vectors from each run as the “test data set” while training the classifier on data from the remaining runs. The average accuracy of all cross-validation steps for each participant reflected how well the patterns of activation within a particular ROI allowed classifying the interpersonal regulation conditions. In other words, this decoding accuracy provided an index for whether this region encoded information about the social proximity between Friend and Stranger.
提取模式向量后，在除一次运行外的所有运行的两种条件（朋友与陌生人）的向量上训练线性支持向量机（SVM），以估计 N 维空间中的决策边界，从而区分与两种人际关系调节条件相关的空间激活模式。这被称为 "训练数据集"。训练好的分类器随后在独立的 "遗漏运行" 数据（即 "测试数据集"）上进行测试。进行六倍交叉验证时，将每次运行的模式向量作为 "测试数据集"，独立重复分类过程，同时用其余运行的数据训练分类器。每位参与者所有交叉验证步骤的平均准确率反映了特定 ROI 内的激活模式对人际关系调节条件的分类效果。换句话说，这一解码准确率提供了一个指标，即该区域是否编码了有关朋友和陌生人之间社交亲疏的信息。

Statistical significance was assessed by testing decoding accuracy values across participants for each ROI (chance level was 50% for two interpersonal conditions) using Bonferroni correction (for the number of ROIs). Note, that using independent cross-validation as well as a selection of ROIs that was not based on a difference contrast between Friend and Stranger conditions circumvented circular inference at any stage of the analyses (Kriegeskorte et al., 2009). The use of the same number of exemplars for each condition and each run guaranteed that both interpersonal regulation conditions were always equally represented in the training and test data.
统计显著性是通过测试每个 ROI 的参与者解码准确率值来评估的（两个人际条件的偶然性水平为 50%），并使用 Bonferroni 校正（针对 ROI 的数量）。需要注意的是，使用独立交叉验证以及非基于朋友和陌生人条件差异对比的 ROI 选择，可以在分析的任何阶段避免循环推断（Kriegeskorte 等人，2009 年）。在每个条件和每次运行中使用相同数量的示例，保证了两种人际关系调节条件在训练和测试数据中始终具有相同的代表性。

3.2. fMRI results 3.2. 核磁共振成像结果

Univariate region-of-interest (ROI) analyses: To identify brain regions within the emotion regulation network that might be modulated by social proximity, we used a set of independent ROIs stemming from a meta-analysis on emotion regulation (Morawetz et al., 2017b) (see Table 1 for details). Betas were extracted from 11 ROIs including the lateral prefrontal cortex (dlPFC, vlPFC, and SFG/MFG), parietal cortex (SMG/TPJ), temporal regions (MTG), SMA, and the amygdala. Using t-tests we compared all task conditions within each ROI after adjusting the α-level for multiple comparisons (pcorr <0.008). Note again that all contrasts were conducted for the actual emotion regulation phase in which only the emotional stimulus was shown and no visual differences between conditions existed.
单变量兴趣区（ROI）分析：为了确定情绪调节网络中可能受社交亲近性调节的脑区，我们使用了一组独立的 ROI，这些 ROI 源自一项关于情绪调节的荟萃分析（Morawetz 等人，2017b）（详见表 1）。我们从 11 个 ROI 提取了 Betas，包括外侧前额叶皮层（dlPFC、vlPFC 和 SFG/MFG）、顶叶皮层（SMG/TPJ）、颞叶区域（MTG）、SMA 和杏仁核。在调整了多重比较的 α 水平后，我们使用 t 检验比较了每个 ROI 中的所有任务条件（p corr < 0.008）。请再次注意，所有对比都是在实际情绪调节阶段进行的，该阶段只显示情绪刺激，不同条件之间不存在视觉差异。

We determined regions with an activation profile (i) representing “general” emotion regulation and cognitive control, i.e. increased activity during regulation independent of the social aspect of the condition compared to the control condition [Decrease+Friend+Stranger > Look; Decrease > Look; Friend > Look; Stranger > Look]; (ii) differentiating between all regulation conditions scaled by social cognition, i.e. showing higher activity for the Friend (social high) and the Stranger (social low) condition compared to the Decrease condition (no social) [Friend+Stranger > Decrease; Friend > Decrease; Stranger > Decrease]; and (iii) relating specifically and only to social proximity, i.e. higher activity in the Stranger compared to the Friend condition, or the other way round, while not strongly activating for intrapersonal regulation compared to the control condition [Stranger > Friend; Friend > Stranger]. In other words, we aimed to categorize the ROIs according to their activation profile: general involvement in emotion regulation (related to system I), social cognition (related to system II), or emotion generation influenced by social proximity (related to system III).
我们确定了具有以下激活特征的区域：(i) 代表 "一般" 情绪调节和认知控制，即与对照条件相比，调节过程中的活动增加，而与调节条件的社会性无关 [减少 + 朋友 + 陌生人> 看；减少>看；朋友>看；陌生人>看]；(ii) 按社会认知程度区分所有调节条件，即与减少条件（无社会性）相比，朋友（社会性高）和陌生人（社会性低）条件下的活动增加 [朋友 + 陌生人> 减少；朋友>减少；陌生人>看]。与减少条件（无社交）相比，朋友条件（社交高）和陌生人条件（社交低）显示出更高的活跃度 [朋友 + 陌生人> 减少；朋友>减少；陌生人>减少]；以及 (iii) 仅与社交接近有关，即与朋友条件相比，陌生人条件显示出更高的活跃度，或者相反，与对照条件相比，人际关系调节没有强烈的活跃度 [陌生人> 朋友；朋友>陌生人]。换句话说，我们的目的是根据 ROI 的激活情况对其进行分类：一般参与情绪调节（与系统 I 有关）、社会认知（与系统 II 有关）或受社会接近性影响的情绪生成（与系统 III 有关）。

The full results are reported in Table 3 and summarised here with respect to the region profiles: Except for two ROIs (right dlPFC and left SFG/MFG), all ROIs demonstrated increased activity during emotion regulation compared to the control condition. However, the activity in bilateral dlPFC, vlPFC, and SMA was not influenced by social aspects of emotion regulation (illustrated in blue in Fig. 2). In contrast, several regions including bilateral SMG, left SFG/MFG, and left MTG demonstrated the highest activity for the Friend condition and lowest activation for the intrapersonal regulation condition, implying an activation profile sensitive to social cognition (illustrated in green in Fig. 2). Notably, neither SMG nor MTG showed significant activation for the intrapersonal regulation condition. Finally, only one region, namely the left amygdala showed the highest activity for the Stranger compared to the Friend condition, thus also demonstrating an activation profile scaling with social proximity, just the opposite direction compared to the left SMG (illustrated in purple in Fig. 2). Results of all t-tests are reported in Table 3. Effect sizes (Cohen's d) of the significant comparisons were medium to large for all ROIs (Table 3). The results of the additional whole-brain analyses are reported in the Supplementary Material (Figs. S1–S2, Tables S2–S4).

Multivariate prediction of social proximity: The univariate whole-brain analysis did not show significant differences between Stranger and Friend conditions (Supplementary Material). In the next step, we aimed to detect social proximity-specific activation patterns within emotion regulation-related regions which were identified in a recent meta-analysis (Decoding analysis I) (Morawetz et al., 2017b). One participant had to be excluded from all MVPA due to only three completed runs which did not allow for a sufficiently powerful cross-validation analysis. We applied MVPA to the cueing phase and the implementation phase. The cueing phase served as a sanity check, as social proximity was introduced by presenting the photo of the stranger or best friend along with specific reappraisal statements. In the implementation phase, however, there were no existing perceptual differences between interpersonal regulation conditions, and neural representations were solely based on internal representations of social proximity.
社会接近性的多变量预测：单变量全脑分析并未显示陌生人和朋友条件下的显著差异（补充材料）。下一步，我们的目标是检测情绪调节相关区域内特定的社交亲近激活模式，这些区域在最近的一项荟萃分析（解码分析 I）中被确定（Morawetz 等人，2017b）。由于只完成了三次运行，无法进行足够强大的交叉验证分析，一名参与者不得不被排除在所有 MVPA 之外。我们将 MVPA 应用于提示阶段和实施阶段。在提示阶段，通过展示陌生人或好友的照片以及特定的再评价语句，引入了社会亲近感，从而起到了理智检查的作用。然而，在实施阶段，人际关系调节条件之间不存在知觉差异，神经表征完全基于社交亲近的内部表征。

In the cueing phase, Friend vs. Stranger could successfully be decoded from bilateral dlPFC and vlPFC, SMA, bilateral SMG, and the right amygdala. Several other regions also showed encoding of social proximity such as left SFG and MTG, but did not survive Bonferroni correction. During the implementation phase, Friend vs. Stranger could successfully be decoded only from the left dlPFC (for all results of Decoding analysis I see Fig. 3A and Table 4). These results demonstrate that in many reappraisal-related regions, including non-visual brain areas, information about social proximity was already represented early, and representations were still present in the lateral prefrontal cortex during emotion regulation.

4. Discussion 4. 讨论

In times of crisis, such as the lockdown during the COVID-19 pandemic, social emotion regulation seems more relevant than ever. In the present study, we investigated whether social support delivered remotely via photos of close friends or strangers accompanied by supporting sentences, helped with downregulating negative emotions. We found that interpersonal emotion regulation was indeed more effective with the help of a friend compared to regulating without support. Additionally, regulating with the support of a stranger was less effective than regulating alone. Importantly, no single brain region alone directly mirrored these specific behavioral results as activation profiles either reflected social vs. non-social conditions or emotion regulation vs. no regulation. Only the SMG/TPJ, SFG/MFG, and the amygdala showed significant differences between Friend and Stranger conditions in the ROI analyses, but several other regions also allowed for decoding these conditions from multivariate patterns. This shows that the reported network as an entity coded for all relevant aspects of the process.
在危机时刻，例如 COVID-19 大流行期间的封锁，社会情绪调节似乎比以往任何时候都更加重要。在本研究中，我们调查了通过亲密朋友或陌生人的照片并配以支持性句子远程提供的社会支持是否有助于负面情绪的调节。我们发现，与没有支持的调节相比，在朋友的帮助下，人际间的情绪调节确实更有效。此外，在陌生人的支持下进行情绪调节的效果也不如单独调节。重要的是，没有一个单独的脑区直接反映了这些特定的行为结果，因为激活特征要么反映了社交条件与非社交条件，要么反映了情绪调节与不调节。在 ROI 分析中，只有 SMG/TPJ、SFG/MFG 和杏仁核在 "朋友" 和 "陌生人" 条件下显示出显著差异，但其他几个区域也能从多元模式中解码这些条件。这表明，所报告的网络作为一个实体，可以对该过程的所有相关方面进行编码。

Most strikingly, we demonstrated that the ability to regulate emotions was modulated by the social support of a friend – even in the absence of physical closeness. This finding is consistent with social baseline theory, suggesting that the friend was perceived as a prosocial other with the goal to help diminish distress, thereby creating a condition of security, social bonding, and closeness (Beckes and Coan, 2011; Coan and Sbarra, 2015). Our questionnaires confirmed that the friends were indeed the individuals that our participants shared emotions with and received support from in everyday life. Hence, the mere knowledge that the reappraisal statement was provided by a close friend seemed to have helped improve participants’ subjective emotional states (Rime, 2009; Wagner et al., 2014). Previous findings on the effectiveness of strangers to help regulate threats are inconsistent. Some studies reported that holding hands with a stranger resulted in a reduced regulation of the brain's response to the shock of threat (Coan et al., 2006; Johnson et al., 2013), while others reported that holding hands with strangers was ineffective (Coan et al., 2017). Our findings suggest that the support of the stranger might have been less effective, potentially because it was perceived as stressful or distracting. Alternatively, participants might have had the most trust in their friend to provide support (our questionnaire results confirmed high levels of trust), followed by high levels of trust in themselves, and the least trust in the stranger.
最引人注目的是，我们证明了调节情绪的能力受到朋友的社会支持的调节，即使在没有身体亲密接触的情况下也是如此。这一发现符合社会基线理论，即朋友被视为亲社会的他人，其目标是帮助减轻痛苦，从而创造一种安全、社会联系和亲近的条件（Beckes 和 Coan，2011 年；Coan 和 Sbarra，2015 年）。我们的问卷调查证实，朋友确实是参与者在日常生活中与之分享情感并从中获得支持的人。因此，只要知道重新评估声明是由亲密朋友提供的，似乎就有助于改善参与者的主观情绪状态（Rime，2009；Wagner 等人，2014）。以往关于陌生人帮助调节威胁的有效性的研究结果并不一致。一些研究报告称，与陌生人牵手会减少大脑对威胁冲击的反应调节（Coan 等人，2006 年；Johnson 等人，2013 年），而另一些研究报告称，与陌生人牵手没有效果（Coan 等人，2017 年）。我们的研究结果表明，陌生人的支持可能不太有效，这可能是因为它被认为是一种压力或分散注意力。另外，参与者可能最信任朋友提供的支持（我们的问卷调查结果证实了高度的信任），其次是对自己的高度信任，而对陌生人的信任度最低。

Our whole-brain fMRI analysis showed increased activity in the left SFG and MTG as well as the precuneus during interpersonal compared to intrapersonal emotion regulation. The same contrast in the ROI analyses revealed enhanced activity in the left dlPFC, bilateral vlPFC, left SMG, left MTG, and bilateral amygdalae. This means that responses in brain regions usually activated during intrapersonal emotion regulation were amplified when social support was provided. The lateral prefrontal and parietal regions play a key role in the cognitive control of emotions and have been associated with working memory, action inhibition, and language processing (Dixon et al., 2017; Kohn et al., 2014; Morawetz et al., 2020). Importantly, none of these activation profiles entirely matched the behavioral finding that the Stranger condition was less effective than intrapersonal regulation. The social regulation conditions also required the processing of a photo in addition to a reappraisal statement. This might have additionally involved working memory functions (Rottschy et al., 2012) and semantic processing (Messina et al., 2015) as the photo and statement would have been kept in mind during the regulation phase, and to use the sentence for regulation, participants would have had to semantically re-process the meaning of the statement.
我们的全脑 fMRI 分析显示，与人际情绪调节相比，人际情绪调节时左侧 SFG 和 MTG 以及楔前肌的活动增加。在 ROI 分析中，同样的对比显示了左侧大脑前区（dlPFC）、双侧 vlPFC、左侧 SMG、左侧 MTG 和双侧杏仁核的活动增强。这说明，在提供社会支持时，通常在个人内部情绪调节过程中被激活的脑区的反应被放大了。外侧前额叶和顶叶区域在情绪的认知控制中起着关键作用，并与工作记忆、行动抑制和语言处理有关（Dixon 等人，2017 年；Kohn 等人，2014 年；Morawetz 等人，2020 年）。重要的是，这些激活特征都不完全符合行为学发现，即陌生人条件比个人内调节更无效。社会调节条件除了重新评估声明外，还需要处理一张照片。这可能会额外涉及工作记忆功能（Rottschy 等人，2012 年）和语义加工（Messina 等人，2015 年），因为在调节阶段，照片和语句会被牢记在心，而为了使用句子进行调节，参与者必须对语句的含义进行语义再加工。

The left SFG/MFG and SMG/TPJ were the only ROIs showing a significant activation increase for the Friend over the Stranger condition. When compared to the Look condition, several regions, including SMG/TPJ, precuneus, SFG, ACC, MTG, and the temporal pole were more strongly activated for the Friend condition on a whole-brain level. These regions have been linked to verbal working memory and semantic processing [MTG, SFG/MFG, temporal pole] (Binder et al., 2009), to reappraisal via perspective-taking, mentalizing and cognitive empathy [SMG/TPJ] (De Waal and Preston, 2017; Jauniaux et al., 2019), socially-driven interactions [ACC] (Lavin et al., 2013), and self-referential processing [precuneus] (Northoff et al., 2006). Taken together, recruitment of these areas, in particular, left TPJ, might support complex social functions required for successful social interactions (Carter and Huettel, 2013; Cavanna and Trimble, 2006) and might reflect the processing of prosocial attributes, e.g., the other's intention and the degree of trust between target and observer (Tusche and Hutcherson, 2018).
左侧 SFG/MFG 和 SMG/TPJ 是唯一在 "朋友" 条件下比 "陌生人" 条件下激活显著增加的 ROI。与 "看" 条件相比，"朋友" 条件下的几个区域，包括 SMG/TPJ、楔前肌、SFG、ACC、MTG 和颞极在全脑水平上被更强地激活。这些区域与言语工作记忆和语义处理 [MTG、SFG/MFG、颞极]（Binder 等人，2009 年）、通过透视、心智化和认知移情进行的重新评估 [SMG/TPJ]（De Waal 和 Preston，2017 年；Jauniaux 等人，2019 年）、社会驱动的互动 [ACC]（Lavin 等人，2013 年）和自我参照处理 [楔前肌]（Northoff 等人，2006 年）有关。综合来看，这些区域的招募，尤其是左侧 TPJ 的招募，可能支持成功社会交往所需的复杂社会功能（Carter 和 Huettel，2013 年；Cavanna 和 Trimble，2006 年），并可能反映亲社会属性的处理，如对方的意图以及目标和观察者之间的信任程度（Tusche 和 Hutcherson，2018 年）。

The amygdala was the only region that was more strongly activated for the Stranger compared to the Friend condition. Others have found attenuated amygdala responses during threat regulation in the presence of a romantic partner versus a friend (Morriss et al., 2019), which supports the idea of decreasing amygdala responses with increasing closeness. The amygdala with its connections to brain networks involved in social cognition appears to be important for the encoding of social connectedness as well as regulating interpersonal distance (Bickart et al., 2014; Kennedy et al., 2009). In this context, it is important to note that although the Stranger condition was related to increased activity in the amygdala during the implementation phase, it was still behaviorally successful (demonstrated in reduced emotional state ratings compared to the Look condition). There are at least three possible interpretations for this observation. First, this could reflect that participants were less at ease as the picture of the stranger might represent a source of distress or distraction, which could explain why the effectiveness of emotion regulation was dampened rather than enhanced when compared to both regulating alone and being supported by the friend. This means that the stranger's support could have been partly helpful, and partly distracting. Second, the amygdala activation might reflect different aspects of trust. Ye (2018) distinguishes between generalized trust and assurance (Ye, 2018). While the friend might have evoked the latter (i.e. the sense of security that arises from a personal relationship), the stranger would only be helpful if it related to a general trust in people, which in turn might be weaker but distinctively reflected in the amygdala activation. In line with this idea, the amygdala has been shown to be implicated in the processing of trustworthiness in faces (Santos et al., 2016), Third, our findings might indicate the degree of difficulty with implementing the regulation strategy, and the amygdala response might indicate that this was more difficult during the Stranger condition than in the other two regulation conditions. Ultimately, follow-up studies are needed to fully understand this pattern of results.
与朋友状态相比，杏仁核是唯一一个在陌生人状态下被更强烈激活的区域。其他研究人员发现，在威胁调节过程中，恋爱伴侣与朋友的杏仁核反应减弱（Morriss 等人，2019），这支持了杏仁核反应随着亲密程度的增加而减弱的观点。杏仁核与涉及社会认知的大脑网络相连，似乎对编码社会联系以及调节人际距离非常重要（Bickart 等人，2014；Kennedy 等人，2009）。在这种情况下，值得注意的是，虽然在实施阶段，"陌生人" 条件与杏仁核活动增加有关，但它在行为上仍然是成功的（表现为与 "看" 条件相比，情绪状态评分降低）。对这一观察结果至少有三种可能的解释。首先，这可能反映出参与者不太自如，因为陌生人的照片可能代表了一种困扰或分心，这就可以解释为什么与单独调节和得到朋友支持相比，情绪调节的效果不是增强了，而是降低了。这意味着陌生人的支持可能部分是有帮助的，部分是分散注意力的。其次，杏仁核激活可能反映了信任的不同方面。Ye（2018）区分了普遍信任和保证（Ye，2018）。朋友可能会唤起后者（即从个人关系中产生的安全感），而陌生人只有在与对人的普遍信任相关时才会有帮助，反过来，这种信任可能较弱，但却明显反映在杏仁核激活中。第三，我们的研究结果可能表明了实施调节策略的困难程度，而杏仁核的反应可能表明，与其他两种调节条件相比，陌生人调节条件下的难度更大。最终，我们还需要进行后续研究来全面了解这种结果模式。

Our MVPA findings of reappraisal-related regions revealed that social proximity was already encoded during the cuing phase in lateral PFC (bilaterally) and parietal regions, and SMA. Importantly, the amygdala was already amongst these regions and could have played a role in processing a threat response, social connectedness, or interpersonal distance. During the implementation phase, the left dlPFC maintained representations of social proximity. Interestingly, social proximity was not only represented in regions that are important for social cognition such as parietal regions and the amygdala (Bickart et al., 2014), but also in lateral PFC regions. It has been suggested that the PFC contributes to regulating emotional responses by representing the value of events in a highly contextualized fashion, i.e. it incorporates complex and abstract information about social context, task rules, self-image, and long-term goals (Cunningham and Zelazo, 2007; Dixon and Christoff, 2014; Ochsner and Gross, 2014). Thus, the representation of the source of social support in the lateral PFC might reflect an explicit appraisal of social context, which in turn might affect the subsequent implementation of the regulation strategy on a neuronal level and, as a consequence, regulatory success.
我们对重新评价相关区域的 MVPA 研究结果表明，在外侧 PFC（双侧）和顶叶区域以及 SMA 中，社交亲疏已经在提示阶段被编码。重要的是，杏仁核已经位于这些区域中，并可能在处理威胁反应、社会联系或人际距离时发挥作用。在实施阶段，左侧脑前部保持着社会亲近感的表征。有趣的是，社会接近性不仅在顶叶区和杏仁核等对社会认知很重要的区域有表征（Bickart 等人，2014 年），而且在侧向前脑功能区也有表征。有研究认为，前额叶功能区通过以高度情境化的方式表征事件的价值来调节情绪反应，即它包含了有关社会环境、任务规则、自我形象和长期目标的复杂而抽象的信息（Cunningham 和 Zelazo, 2007; Dixon 和 Christoff, 2014; Ochsner 和 Gross, 2014）。因此，外侧前脑功能区对社会支持来源的表征可能反映了对社会背景的明确评估，这反过来又可能影响神经元水平上调节策略的后续实施，从而影响调节的成功与否。

In addition, regions found to be implicated in interpersonal regulation such as the precuneus, left SFG and MTG also encoded social relevant information at an early stage. The precuneus further represented social proximity during the implementation phase. This set of brain regions might have supported mentalizing and processing social aspects such as affiliation and connectedness (Bickart et al., 2014; Jauniaux et al., 2019). In particular, the precuneus is suggested to play a crucial role in encoding information of the source of social support by first- versus third-person perspective taking, perceived agency, and social cognition (Cavanna and Trimble, 2006).
此外，楔前肌、左侧 SFG 和 MTG 等被发现与人际关系调节有关的区域也在早期阶段编码了与社会相关的信息。在实施阶段，楔前肌进一步代表了社会接近性。这组脑区可能支持心智化和处理社会方面的问题，如从属关系和联系（Bickart 等人，2014；Jauniaux 等人，2019）。特别是，楔前肌被认为在通过第一人称视角与第三人称视角、感知机构和社会认知编码社会支持来源信息方面发挥着至关重要的作用（Cavanna 和 Trimble，2006 年）。

Taken together, our findings demonstrate that the enhanced ability to regulate negative emotions through the support of a close friend might be based on an interplay of different large-scale brain networks (e.g., emotion regulation, social cognition, and cognitive control), rather than the recruitment of a single brain region (Barrett and Satpute, 2013). Some parts of the cognitive control network based on lateral PFC regions were more activated for the presence of an external person but seemed to be indifferent to the source of the support. This suggests a role in compensating for increased processing demands related to understanding sentences and pictures, and working memory. The social cognition network might support empathic and mentalizing processes during interpersonal regulation to encode the social aspects of the provided reappraisals at large. Only some regions within this network explicitly encoded who provided the social support, either by increased (TPJ) or decreased (amygdala) activation differences, or through distinct patterns (e.g., precuneus). Reappraisal has been suggested to be implemented by the cognitive control network employing a top-down process which down-regulates the activity in the emotion network during successful regulation (Johnstone et al., 2007; Urry et al., 2006). Our results suggest that during interpersonal regulation, the social cognition network as well as regions supporting self-referential processing and emotional stress responses might contribute important information, which can facilitate (for friends) or interfere (for strangers) with regulation success. Future studies need to elucidate this interplay between networks during interpersonal emotion regulation, e.g., by implementing effective connectivity analysis and determine potential trait and situational moderators that influence regulation ability.
总之，我们的研究结果表明，通过亲密朋友的支持来增强调节负面情绪的能力可能是基于不同大规模大脑网络（如情绪调节、社会认知和认知控制）的相互作用，而不是单一大脑区域的招募（Barrett 和 Satpute，2013 年）。认知控制网络的某些部分以外侧前脑功能区为基础，在外部人员出现时被更多激活，但似乎对支持的来源漠不关心。这表明，在理解句子和图片以及工作记忆等相关处理需求增加的情况下，社会认知网络起到了补偿作用。社会认知网络可能支持人际调节过程中的移情和心智化过程，以编码所提供的重新评价的社会方面。该网络中只有部分区域明确编码了谁提供了社会支持，这些区域或通过激活差异的增加（TPJ）或减少（杏仁核），或通过不同的模式（如楔前肌）。重新评估被认为是由认知控制网络实施的一个自上而下的过程，该过程会在成功调节期间下调情绪网络的活动（Johnstone 等人，2007 年；Urry 等人，2006 年）。我们的研究结果表明，在人际调节过程中，社会认知网络以及支持自我参照处理和情绪压力反应的区域可能会贡献重要的信息，这些信息可能会促进（对朋友而言）或干扰（对陌生人而言）调节的成功。未来的研究需要阐明人际情绪调节过程中网络之间的相互作用，例如，通过实施有效的连通性分析，确定影响调节能力的潜在特质和情境调节因子。

5. Limitations & outlook 5. 局限与展望

Several limitations should be noted. First, it is likely that decoding accuracies in the cueing phase also reflect visual differences between conditions with respect to photos and sentences; however, the substantial spread of information through regions that are beyond the visual system, including brain areas strongly involved in self-referential processing and emotion regulation, suggests the presence of more abstract representations of social proximity that could have been integrated to influence the regulation process more directly. Our paradigm did not allow us to fully dissociate the processing of the visual information from its semantic meaning during the cueing phase.
有几个局限性值得注意。首先，提示阶段的解码准确率很可能也反映了不同条件下照片和句子的视觉差异；然而，信息在视觉系统以外区域的大量传播，包括强烈参与自我参照处理和情绪调节的脑区，表明存在更抽象的社会接近性表征，这些表征本可以被整合起来，更直接地影响调节过程。我们的研究范式无法让我们在提示阶段将视觉信息的处理与其语义完全分离开来。

There might be other consequences of showing different visual content during the cueing phase of the intra- and interpersonal regulation conditions. The presence of sentences or images could have impacted emotion regulation (and the neural processing in the following implementation phase) to some degree. We made sure that the implementation phase – which was analysed in our study – was visually identical between the regulation conditions (only the emotional image was presented), and that it was possible to estimate neural signals independent from the cueing phase by using appropriately jittered fixation periods separating them. Nevertheless, it remains a possibility that some advantage (or disadvantage) could be derived from seeing faces and/or sentences before entering the implementation phase. Related to this, it is important to note that in the interpersonal conditions, the reappraisal sentences were provided to the participants while these had to be self-generated in the intrapersonal condition. This means that the re-interpretation of the stimuli might have been less effortful in the interpersonal conditions. However, we did not observe an overall advantage of the interpersonal conditions in the behavioural patterns that would be congruent with this idea, as the intrapersonal condition (no faces, no sentences) was indeed less effective than the Friend condition but more effective than the Stranger condition (both of which had faces and sentences). This of course does not negate that due to the existing differences, intra- and interpersonal conditions would have differed qualitatively in the cognitive processes involved, which would necessarily to some extent engage different neural systems. For example, the intrapersonal condition required keeping in mind the abstract regulation goal followed by a translation of this goal into a verbal self-instruction. The same was not the case in the interpersonal conditions, which required participants to keep in mind an exact sentence that would be used during regulation. Future studies could address some of these shortcomings by introducing irrelevant pictures and sentences in the intrapersonal condition; however, these might then exert an unwanted distracting influence on emotion regulation and potentially introduce a new confound. Some other differences – i.e. the differences in memory load and content for inter- vs. intrapersonal regulation – are inherent to the processes under investigation and not avoidable (i.e. intrapersonal regulation simply rules out that regulatory sentences are provided; interpersonal regulation rules out that these are self-generated). These aspects, however, should be kept in mind when interpreting differences in neural patterns.
在内部调节和人际调节条件的提示阶段显示不同的视觉内容可能会产生其他后果。句子或图像的出现可能会在一定程度上影响情绪调节（以及接下来实施阶段的神经处理）。我们确保在我们的研究中分析的实施阶段在视觉上与调节条件完全相同（只呈现情绪图像），并且通过使用适当的抖动固定期将两者分开，可以估算出独立于提示阶段的神经信号。尽管如此，在进入执行阶段之前看到面孔和 / 或句子仍有可能带来一些优势（或劣势）。与此相关，需要注意的是，在人际关系条件下，重新评价句子是提供给被试的，而在人际关系条件下，这些句子必须由被试自己生成。这意味着，在人际关系条件下，对刺激的重新解释可能会更省力。然而，我们并没有观察到人际关系条件在行为模式上的整体优势与这一观点相一致，因为人际关系条件（无面孔、无句子）确实不如朋友关系条件有效，但却比陌生人关系条件有效（两者都有面孔和句子）。当然，这并不能否认，由于存在差异，人内条件和人际条件所涉及的认知过程会有质的不同，这必然会在一定程度上调动不同的神经系统。例如，人内条件要求牢记抽象的调节目标，然后将这一目标转化为口头自我指令。而人际交往条件则不同，它要求参与者牢记在调节过程中会用到的准确句子。未来的研究可以通过在人际条件中引入无关的图片和句子来解决其中的一些缺陷；但是，这些图片和句子可能会对情绪调节产生不必要的干扰影响，并有可能引入新的混淆因素。其他一些差异 -- 即人与人之间的调节与人与人之间的调节在记忆负荷和内容上的差异 -- 是调查过程中固有的，无法避免（即人与人之间的调节只是排除了提供调节句子的可能性；而人与人之间的调节则排除了这些句子是自己产生的可能性）。不过，在解释神经模式的差异时，应牢记这些方面。

Second, during the implementation phase, participants were asked to down-regulate their emotions using reappraisal. However, we did not tightly control whether they always used the instructed sentences during the interpersonal regulation conditions, nor could we assess whether self-driven regulation in the intrapersonal regulation condition always involved reappraisal. Notably, participants did not report having used other strategies, but future experiments could probe compliance at multiple time points during the experiment. Third, in the current experiment, the Look condition was not completely matched with the interpersonal regulation conditions during the cueing phase. Thus, it would be of great interest to compare the Friend and Stranger condition to another baseline such as presenting the photo of the friend or stranger without the need for regulation, which would optimize the current Look condition.
其次，在实施阶段，我们要求受试者通过重新评估来降低自己的情绪。但是，我们并没有严格控制参与者在人际调节条件下是否总是使用指导句，也无法评估在人际调节条件下自我驱动调节是否总是涉及重新评估。值得注意的是，参与者并没有报告使用过其他策略，但未来的实验可以在实验过程中的多个时间点对依从性进行调查。第三，在目前的实验中，在提示阶段，观察条件与人际关系调节条件并不完全匹配。因此，将 "朋友和陌生人" 条件与另一种基线条件进行比较将是非常有意义的，例如在不需要调节的情况下展示朋友或陌生人的照片，这将优化当前的 "看" 条件。

Future studies could also further explore how levels of trust, in particular when ascribing competency in providing emotional support, might moderate the effectiveness of social support. A stranger, who is trusted because of high levels of perceived competency (e.g., a mental health professional), might have a much more positive influence on emotion regulation than the random stranger in our study.
未来的研究还可以进一步探讨信任度，尤其是在赋予提供情感支持的能力时，信任度会如何调节社会支持的有效性。与我们研究中随机出现的陌生人相比，因感知能力高而受到信任的陌生人（如心理健康专家）可能会对情绪调节产生更积极的影响。

6. Conclusion 6. 结论

In conclusion, our results suggest that social proximity represents an important factor for the effective implementation of social support in emotionally challenging situations that, in turn, is based upon the interplay of lateral prefrontal and subcortical networks that are important for cognitive control, integration of social information, self-referential processing, and the generation of emotional responses. The current results highlight the power of strong social connectedness that has the potential to boost our emotion regulation ability and thus to contribute to our social and emotional well-being (Sandstrom and Dunn, 2014).
总之，我们的研究结果表明，社会接近性是在具有情感挑战的情况下有效实施社会支持的一个重要因素，而社会接近性又是建立在外侧前额叶和皮层下网络相互作用的基础上的，这些网络对于认知控制、社会信息整合、自我参照处理和情感反应的产生都很重要。目前的研究结果凸显了强大的社会联系的力量，它有可能提高我们的情绪调节能力，从而促进我们的社会和情感福祉（Sandstrom 和 Dunn，2014 年）。
